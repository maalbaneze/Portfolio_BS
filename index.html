About Me

Father of three grown children, husband, 30-year Army veteran and retiree, and amateur photographer with a special interest in underwater photography of marine life. My wife's name is Mary Beth---so nice they named her twice! We have one cat (Buddy), and a part-time dog (Cassie) that we share with the family that takes care of her most of the time.

During my military career, I deployed several times to serve the Nation. I was serving at the Pentagon on 9/11 and the hijackled jet flew right underneath my office. I've had the good fortune over my lifetime of visiting 42 countries, and I hope to add to this list in the coming years!
